mask

(work in progress)
Creation of a mask which becomes the allegory of my personnality. The mask reveals who we are but it can also hide what we don't want to show. This project is still in process and it will include VR and motion design soon.

The project started by getting out of myself to look at me from the outside and draw my own portrait. Then I decided to highlight my mysterious side and the fact that I am an introvert person who rarely give free acces to her emotions and soul. Therefore I imagined a stiff and opaque black origamic shape which evokes a rough stone with no shade or reflection. From the outside, it looks lifeless and boring but if you dig in, you will discover another universe made of lights and bright colours, surprising and floating.
